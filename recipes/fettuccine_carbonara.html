Fettuccine Carbonara

Description

This fettuccine carbonara is a delectable, mouth-watering pile of yummy goodness! I recommend a nice salad with it — that's all you will need for a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • 5 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1 pound bacon, cut into strips
  • 1 (16 ounce) package dry fettuccine pasta
  • ¾ cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 3 egg yolks
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

    Step 1

  1. Heat olive oil in a large heavy saucepan over medium heat. Stir in bacon cook and stir until bacon is evenly browned.Remove from heat.

    2. Step 2

  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente. Drain pasta, then return it to the pot.

    3. Step 3

  3. Whisk Parmesan and egg yolks together in a medium bowl. Pour bacon mixture over pasta stir in cream mixture. Season with salt and pepper.
